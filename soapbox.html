SoapBox Website

SoapBox is an employee engagement and innovation platform based in Toronto. We worked with their designer to build their marketing website according to SoapBox's established brand identity. The end-product featured a Wordpress-powered CMS, a blog, HubSpot integration for email sales subscriptions, User Analytics, and A/B testing capabilities.
